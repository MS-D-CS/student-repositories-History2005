PENUT BUTTER KISS COOKIES

These cookies are really easy to make. When you finish them you will be wanting to make more. Dont cut butter into dry ingredients it will result in tuny bits of flour-coated butter throughout the dough. If you dont have a pastry blender, use a knife to cut into the butter.

EQUIPMENT LIST

  • baking sheet
  • Large Mixxing Bowl
  • pastry blender or knife
  • smaller Mixxing bowl
